About Us

When we built BrightStorm, our goal was to give grade 5 and 6 students the information and ability to learn the things they need. We set out to create a new, interactive way of learning — making it engaging, flexible, and accessible for as many people as possible. We strive to improve the students' well-being with regards to learning in this time of pandemic.

What is BrightStorm

BrightStorm believes passionately that learning have the power to change attitudes, perspective in life, and our lives as a whole. This underlying philosophy is the driving force behind all of BrightStorm's endeavors in putting together the best learning materials and accurate information for the students. With this philosophy in mind, and with the intention of supporting the students and sparking the curiosity of learners, BrightStorm was built.

Our mission

We want to create a website where anyone can learn something insightful, where everyone learns sufficient information, provided with various resources, and opportunities to do so. BrightStorm contains a world of possibilities — all that’s required is the curiosity and drive to learn. At BrightStorm, we are committed to empowering all students, especially grade 5 and 6 students in their learning journey, to continue to learn, grow, and make an impact on the world around them.
